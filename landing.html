Telecom Churn Analysis

With growth in the telecom industry and increasing competition it has become extremely difficult to offer great deals to the customer, while maintaining profits and being a strong competitor among other competitors in the industry. To do so we need to understand customer behavior and other factors which contribute to retaining customers. On Telecom Dataset performed EDA including Feature selection, Collinearity check, Outlier, Imputation, Data Scaling and Prediction with and without outlier using KNNto understand which features contribute to retain customers.

Penguin Species Detection

The Palmer Penguins dataset contains size measurements for three penguin species observed on three islands in the Palmer Archipelago, Antarctica. The goal was to classify different penguin species based on different features of the penguin. Used AutoML model called Pycaret, we performed MultiClass classification. Along with this Binary classification was perform to detect the sex and Regression was performed to predict the culmen length.
